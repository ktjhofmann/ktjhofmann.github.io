Simple Crêpes

This recipe is ideal if you look for a simple way to enrich your Sunday breakfast table or for a quick and delicious desert. I bet you have all ingredients in your pantry! The crêpes go well with agave syrup, fresh fruit or any sweet or savory topping you have at hand. The ingredients below are for 2 portions.

Ingredients

1 egg
1 tablespoon sugar
1 cup milk
3/4 cups flour
pinch of salt

Method

In a mixing bowl, add the egg and sugar and whisk until fluffy. Mix in the milk.

Add the flour to the mixing bowl. If you have a fine mesh strainer, sift the flour. Add the salt and mix the ingredients well.

Let the dough rest for up to 15 minutes. Bake the crêpes in a nonstick pan over medium-high heat.
