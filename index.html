Welcome to my personal website

Thanks for visiting my website!

I am currently a predoctoral research fellow at the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research (SIEPR), working for Petra Persson and Maria Polyakova. I obtained my undergraduate and Master's degrees at the University of Mannheim, Germany, and am going to start my PhD in Economics at Stanford University this fall. For more information about my professional background, you can visit my LinkedIn or access my CV.

Work in progress

I am co-author of two studies in health policy. The first project is an international collaboration that compares the relationship between income and life expectancy in ten high-income countries, building on the work by Chetty et al. (2016). The second project examines intergenerational social mobility and occupational heritability in medicine.

Code repository

For many years, I have been working with Stata and R. Below, I am sharing my small respository of code snippets and programs, that I discovered online or wrote myself and that have proved useful in numerous research tasks.

Cooking and baking

In urgent need of new hobbies practiced indoors — thanks COVID-19, I rediscovered my love for food "fait à la maison". Growing up, cooking was a fundamental part of family life, and I want to collect and share recipes that remind me of home, that stuck with me over the years, and that I learned along the way. All recipes are vegetarian, some are vegan. None of the recipes are particularly healthy, but all are exceptionally good.
