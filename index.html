Katja Hofmann

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Stanford. I study the industrial organization of health care markets, fascinated by how regulatory changes shape the horizontal and vertical relationships between firms and patients. My research focuses on the United States, but I am always curious to learn about new settings and explore novel data sources.
